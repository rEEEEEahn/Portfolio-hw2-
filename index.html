About Me

Hello! My name is Ryan Hsiao and I am a Web Developer! I was born in Taipei, Taiwan but raised in sunny San Diego. I love to stay active yet love to play video games. I went to school for Communication but did not know what I wanted to do with it. For a few years after, I bounced around different professions from the food industry to car dealerships; I was happy but I always felt I could do more.

After the pandemic, my friend talked to me about his experiences with the USCD web development course and how he has better himself from the course. After months of back-and-forth, I decided to enroll and now I am a current student of the USCD web development boot camp! Here you will find some of my work from the course. Hope you can follow me on my journey as I learn and get better and better!
